tiktok events

The premise is simple: find a way for Eurovision fans to enjoy the show from afar, through TikTok. From the information I gathered and the people I interviewed, one thing is clear: they have a certain routine/tradition they do every year with their family or friends, and in the year 2020, they missed it.
