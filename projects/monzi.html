The client: Monzi was born out of Monica Barculescu’s love for fashion, creativity and motherhood. It is a toddlers’ accessory and fashion brand based in Suceava, Romania.

My work: In late spring of 2021, Monica reached out to me and asked me to help her create her logo and tagline for her newly launched brand. We brainstormed together based on the color palette she had in mind, and after sketching and digitalizing, we reached a lovely end result.

You can follow her work here: https://monzi.ro
