iafk/kabk open day

Project made in collaboration with Daria Brandenburg (https://dariadbrandenburg.myportfolio.com).

In January of 2019, during my gap year at the Royal Academy of Arts The Hague (KABK), we took on the task of designing and deciding the visual identity for the Open Day for the Interior Architecture department.

I played with a picture I took on a day when we were exploring the academy building and ended up in a corner we were not supposed to, by opening a locked door, and we liked what came out of it so we decided to turn it into the posters.

We decided on the color palette and then bumped into the problem of printing, and decided it was time to experiment with silk-screen printing, which we did.
