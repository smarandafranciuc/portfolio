Whiteout, 2016

hand-drawn interior perspective
of a pristine home that brings
the desert to the mountain
the main idea was that the house
would present a typical apparent shell
and the interior would be bright and
unexpected for the common
viewer, while still hosting
its family;
it's based on a longing
feeling of warmth in the
middle of the winter

Artificial Walls, 2016

scale model for an apartment project
in a post-soviet flat
in theory, the beneficiary would be a
Wiccan couple.
the main idea of this apartment was
blocking the natural light with
a black wall, in which small holes were poked
to let rays in;
every light in the space would be
artificial, except for the
1m wide space between the wall and
the original windows
